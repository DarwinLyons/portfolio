About

Hi! My name is Darwin Lyons. I’m an actor, director, creator, educator and producer in theatre and film. I make art in the hopes that audiences will be transported to a different world, and then be left with questions about what it means to be human. My favourite things are funny things, kind things, andinteresting things.
